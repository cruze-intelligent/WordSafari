## Animal Asset Sources

Most animal PNGs in this folder are sourced from FreePixel packs:

- `Pixel Art Animals Vol 1: Land`
- `Pixel Art Animals Vol 2: Sea, Sky & Bugs`

License in both pack `README.txt` files: free to use in projects, credit appreciated but not required.

Two exact matches are sourced directly from the FreePixel animals catalog:

- `python.png` -> `snake-python-coiled-brown-pattern_20260217_091529.png`
- `clownfish.png` -> `clownfish-orange-white-stripes-tropical_20260217_092445.png`

The following animals now use species-specific handcrafted SVG fallbacks instead of inaccurate substitute PNGs:

- `arctic_fox.svg`
- `fennec.svg`
- `meerkat.svg`
- `horned_lizard.svg`
- `roadrunner.svg`

Two pack assets were also upgraded to cleaner variants:

- `lion.png`
- `zebra.png`
